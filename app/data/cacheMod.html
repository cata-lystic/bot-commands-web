JREbot Commands

All commands begin with the . (period) prefix. Most commands can only be used in the #bots channel.

Audio

bump Bump a track number to the top of queue

bumpplay Force play a URL or search for a track

disconnect Disconnect from the voice channel

lyrics playing Lyrics of currently playing song

lyrics search Search lyrics song

now Now playing

pause Pause or resume a playing track

play Play a song from YouTube (search or URL)

prev Skip to the start of the previously played track

queue List songs in queue

remove Remove a specific track number from the queue

repeat Toggle repeat

search Pick a track with a search

seek Seek ahead/behind by seconds or to a specific time

shuffle Toggle shuffle

skip Skip to the next track, or a given track number

stop Stop playback and clear queue

volume Sets the volume (1%-150%)

Crypto

btc Convert from BTC to given currency

coins Show chart of top crypto coins

stock Show current ticker symbol price

xmr Converts from XMR to given currency
